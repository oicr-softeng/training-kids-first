 The third Genomics and Cloud Technology Alliance Conference 2018 (GCTA Conference) will be held on October 26, 2018, in the National Gene Bank of Shenzhen. GCTA aims to explore the status quo of the industry, interpret technical standards, and solve application problems by exploring the topic of bioinformation cloud computing technology and industrial development. Scientific research, technology development, and industrial services in the field of computing are combined to promote exchanges and innovations in the field of bioinformation cloud computing.
